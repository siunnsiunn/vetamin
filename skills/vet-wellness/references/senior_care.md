# Feline Senior Care (AAFP 2021)

## Senior vs. Geriatric vs. Frailty
- **Senior**: Age-based (>10 years).
- **Geriatric**: Health status (no specific age).
- **Frailty**: Syndrome of decreased functional reserve, leading to vulnerability.

## CDS Screening (DISHA-AL)
- **D**isorientation (confusion, forgetfulness).
- **I**nteractions (changes with people/pets).
- **S**leep-wake cycle (nighttime vocalization).
- **H**ouse-soiling (outside litter box).
- **A**ctivity (decreased play/grooming).
- **A**nxiety (aloof or clingy).
- **L**earning/Memory.

## Common Comorbidities
- CKD + Hyperthyroidism (Treatment of FHT can unmask CKD).
- CKD + DJD (NSAID use requires caution).
- Hyperthyroidism + Diabetes Mellitus.
- Obesity + DJD/DM/Cardiac.

## Quality of Life (QOL) & End of Life
- **Budgets of Care**: Financial, Time, Emotional, Physical.
- **Palliative Care**: Focus on comfort (pain, mobility, nutrition).
- **Hospice Care**: specialized end-of-life care bridging to euthanasia.
