# Referral and Community Care Guidelines

## Referral Principles
- **Collaborative Care**: Sharing care between primary and specialty teams.
- **Hands-on Referral**: SCT assumes role of Directing Veterinarian (DV) for the specific condition.
- **Referral Roadmap**: Clear communication on how to initiate, what records are needed, and approximate timelines.

## Family-Centered Practice
- **Contextualized Care**: Considers family's particular needs, goals, and challenges (transportation, financial).
- **Informed Decision-making**: Discuss quality-of-life measures and points for reassessment.

## Community Care
- **Goal**: Keeping pets with families by increasing access to care.
- **Access to Care**: Affordable and available services regardless of race, socioeconomic status, etc.
- **Redefined "Gold Standard"**: Integrating spectrum of care (SOC), listening to pet caregiver needs, and ensuring high-quality service.

## Communication with Clients
- Explain spectrum of options (not just the most expensive).
- Be transparent about costs.
- Use "chunking and checking" to avoid information overload (manage illness uncertainty).
