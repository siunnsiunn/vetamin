# Veterinary Lab Cross-Interpretation (LAB_CROSS.md)

## 1. Renal Pattern (Based on IRIS & ISFM Guidelines)
- **Azotemia (High CREA/BUN)** + **Low USG (<1.035 in cats, <1.030 in dogs)**: 
    - Pattern: Primary Renal Disease.
    - Check: BP, UPC, SDMA.
- **Azotemia** + **High USG**: 
    - Pattern: Pre-renal Azotemia (Dehydration).
- **Staging (IRIS)**:
    - Stage 1: CREA < 140 (Cat) / < 125 (Dog) with other evidence.
    - Stage 2: CREA 140-250 (Cat) / 125-250 (Dog).
    - Stage 3: CREA 251-440 (Cat) / 251-440 (Dog).
    - Stage 4: CREA > 440 (Both).

## 2. Liver & NSAID Monitoring (Based on AAHA/AAFP)
- **ALT/AST > 2-3x Upper Normal Limit**: 
    - Red Flag: Stop NSAIDs immediately.
    - Check: Bile acids, Albumin, Bilirubin.
- **High ALKP + High GGT (Cat)**: 
    - Pattern: Cholestasis / Hepatic Lipidosis.

## 3. Cardiac Screening (Based on ISFM)
- **High NT-proBNP**: 
    - Red Flag: Asymptomatic heart disease or CHF risk.
    - Check: Echocardiography, Thoracic Radiography.
