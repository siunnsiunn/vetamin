# BSAVA Electrolytes & Acid-base Interpretation (ELECTROLYTES.md)

## 1. Sodium & Potassium (Na/K)
- **Na/K Ratio < 27**: 強烈懷疑 **Hypoadrenocorticism (Addison's Disease)**。
- **Other causes of Low Na/K**: 嚴重鞭蟲感染 (Trichuris), 腹水 (Chylothorax), 腎衰竭急性期。

## 2. Calcium (Ca) & Phosphorus (PHOS)
- **Corrected Calcium (Dog)**: 
    - Formula: $Ca_{corrected} = Ca_{measured} - ALB + 3.5$.
- **Hypercalcaemia Differentials (HOGS IN YARD)**:
    - **H**yperparathyroidism (Primary).
    - **O**steolytic disease.
    - **G**ranulomatous disease.
    - **S**purious (Lipemia).
    - **I**diopathic (Cat - 貓咪最常見).
    - **N**eoplasia (LSA, ASGAC - 最具臨床意義).
    - **Y**oung (Normal growth).
    - **A**ddison's.
    - **R**enal failure.
    - **D**-vitamin toxicity (滅鼠藥).

## 3. Chloride (Cl) Correction
- **Formula**: $Cl_{corrected} = Cl_{measured} \times (Na_{normal} / Na_{measured})$.
- **Note**: 校正後的 Cl 異常代表原發性的酸鹼代謝問題 (Metabolic Acidosis/Alkalosis)。
