# Electrolytes & Acid-Base Interpretation

## 1. Key Markers

### Potassium (K+)
- **Distribution**: Primarily intracellular (140-150 mmol/l)。Serum levels 被嚴格調控 (Dogs 3.5-5.8, Cats 3.6-4.5 mmol/l)。
- **Hyperkalaemia**:
  - **Pseudohyperkalaemia**: Haemolysis (特別是 Akitas), thrombocytosis, massive leucocytosis, EDTA contamination。
  - **Reduced Excretion**: Anuric/oliguric renal failure, urinary obstruction (FLUTD), uroabdomen (ruptured bladder), Hypoadrenocorticism (Addison's disease)。
  - **Redistribution**: Metabolic acidosis (inorganic acids 會將細胞內的 K+ 交換出 H+), massive tissue breakdown (tumour lysis syndrome, reperfusion injury, trauma)。
- **Hypokalaemia**:
  - **Decreased Intake**: Anorexia。
  - **Increased Loss**: Vomiting/diarrhoea, chronic renal failure (CRF - 貓極常見), loop/thiazide diuretics, Hyperaldosteronism (Conn's syndrome)。
  - **Redistribution**: Alkalosis, insulin/glucose therapy (將 K+ drive into cells)。
  - **Feline Specifics**: Hypokalaemic myopathy (Burmese cats, CRF cats) 會造成 cervical ventroflexion 與 muscle weakness。

### Sodium (Na+)
- **Hypernatraemia**:
  - **Water loss > Na loss**: Hypotonic fluid loss (GI loss, osmotic diuresis), Diabetes insipidus (central/nephrogenic), lack of access to water, heatstroke。
  - **Excess Na gain**: Salt poisoning, hypertonic saline administration。
- **Hyponatraemia**:
  - **Pseudohyponatraemia**: Marked hyperlipidaemia 或 hyperproteinaemia (使用 indirect ISE methods 時)。
  - **Na loss**: Hypoadrenocorticism, GI loss, 3rd spacing (effusions), diuretics。
  - **Dilutional (Volume Overload)**: Congestive heart failure (CHF), advanced hepatic/renal failure, nephrotic syndrome。
  - **Hyperosmolar shift**: Diabetes mellitus (hyperglycaemia 將水份 draw into intravascular space)。

### Chloride (Cl-)
- 通常與 Sodium 變動趨勢一致以維持 electroneutrality。
- **Pseudohyperchloraemia**: Potassium bromide therapy (ISE 會將 Br- 誤讀為 Cl-)。
- **Hypochloraemia (with normal Na+)**: Metabolic alkalosis 的 hallmark (例如 severe vomiting 導致 gastric acid content 丟失)。

### Magnesium (Mg2+)
- Na+/K+ ATPase pump 的重要 cofactor。
- **Hypomagnesaemia**: 會導致 refractory hypokalaemia (K+ supplementation 無效，直到 Mg2+ 被 corrected) 以及 cardiac arrhythmias。

## 2. Clinical Patterns

### 2.1 Low Na:K Ratio (< 27, strictly < 24)
- **Primary Hypoadrenocorticism (Addison's Disease)**: Lack of aldosterone 導致 Na+ loss 與 K+ retention。
- **Pseudohypoadrenocorticism**:
  - Severe gastrointestinal disease (如 Trichuris vulpis / whipworm infection)。
  - Renal failure (oliguric / anuric)。
  - Third-space fluid loss (如 chylothorax with repeated drainage, uroabdomen)。

### 2.2 Feline Hypokalaemic Myopathy
- **Presenting signs**: Plantigrade stance, cervical ventroflexion, muscle weakness, elevated CK。
- **Differentials**: Chronic renal failure (CRF), Primary Hyperaldosteronism (Conn's syndrome，常併發 systemic hypertension), Burmese hypokalaemic myopathy。

### 2.3 Hypercalcaemia
- **Differentials (HOGS IN YARD)**:
  - **H**yperparathyroidism (Primary).
  - **O**steolytic disease.
  - **G**ranulomatous disease (如 fungal, macrophage activation).
  - **S**purious (Lipemia, lab error, EDTA contamination 會降低 Ca，但 lipemia 可能 falsely elevate total Ca).
  - **I**diopathic (Cats - 貓最常見的原因).
  - **N**eoplasia (Lymphoma, Anal Sac Gland Adenocarcinoma - 狗最常見且 clinically significant).
  - **Y**oung (Normal growth).
  - **A**ddison's disease.
  - **R**enal failure (通常 associated with normal to low ionized calcium，但 total Ca 可能 increased).
  - **D**-vitamin toxicity (Rodenticides, cholecalciferol).

## 3. Formulas & Corrections

### 3.1 Corrected Calcium (Dog)
- **Formula**: $Ca_{corrected} (mg/dL) = Ca_{measured} (mg/dL) - ALB (g/dL) + 3.5$
- **Purpose**: 校正 ALB 變化對 total calcium 的影響。**注意**: 測量 ionized calcium (iCa) 永遠優於 calculated corrections。

### 3.2 Chloride Correction
- **Formula**: $Cl_{corrected} = Cl_{measured} \times (Na_{normal} / Na_{measured})$
- **Purpose**: 識別 primary acid-base disturbances。若 corrected Cl- 異常，代表存在 primary metabolic acidosis (elevated Cl) 或 alkalosis (low Cl)，與 free water changes 無關。
