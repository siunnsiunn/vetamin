# BSAVA Erythrocyte Interpretation (ERYTHROCYTES.md)

## 1. Anemia Classification (貧血分類)
- **Regenerative (再生性)**: 
    - Reticulocytes > 60,000/µL (Cat) / > 80,000/µL (Dog).
    - **Differentials**: Hemorrhage (血外流), Hemolysis (溶血 - IMHA, Parasites).
- **Non-regenerative (非再生性)**:
    - **Differentials**: Chronic Disease (發炎/腫瘤), Renal Disease (EPO ↓), Iron Deficiency (慢性失血長期演變), Bone Marrow Disease.

## 2. RBC Indices Patterns
- **Macrocytic Hypochromic (MCV ↑, MCHC ↓)**: 典型強烈再生性反應。
- **Microcytic Hypochromic (MCV ↓, MCHC ↓)**: 典型 **Iron Deficiency** (鐵缺乏)。
- **Normocytic Normochromic**: 慢性病引發或早期急性貧血。

## 3. Polycythemia (多血症)
- **Relative**: Dehydration (HCT ↑ + TP ↑).
- **Absolute Primary**: Polycythemia Vera.
- **Absolute Secondary**: Hypoxia (肺病/心臟病) 或 EPO-producing tumors.
