# Hepatic Disease Interpretation

## 1. Key Markers

### Hepatocellular Leakage Enzymes
- **ALT (Alanine Aminotransferase)**:
  - **Localization**: Cytosolic.
  - **Half-life**: Dogs ~3 days; Cats < 1 day (貓代謝極快，輕微上升即具 clinical significance).
  - **Characteristics**: 評估 hepatocellular damage 的 sensitive marker.
  - **Interferences / Secondary**: Reactive hepatopathies (如 GI inflammation, hypoxia, severe dental disease), Hyperthyroidism (cats), Drug-induced (如 corticosteroids, phenobarbital).
- **AST (Aspartate Aminotransferase)**:
  - **Localization**: Cytosolic & Mitochondrial.
  - **Half-life**: Dogs ~5 hours; Cats ~1 hour.
  - **Characteristics**: 代表 deeper 或 more severe necrosis。Not liver-specific (muscle 與 RBCs 也有)。若懷疑 muscle damage，需 check CK (Creatine Kinase)。
  - **Feline Note**: 在貓，被認為是比 ALT 更敏感的 significant liver disease marker。

### Biliary / Cholestatic Marker Enzymes
- **ALKP / ALP (Alkaline Phosphatase)**:
  - **Half-life**: Dogs ~3 days; Cats ~6 hours.
  - **Characteristics**: 膽汁淤積 (Cholestasis) 或藥物誘導 (de novo synthesis) 而上升。
  - **Isoenzymes**:
    - L-ALP (Liver): Cholestasis.
    - B-ALP (Bone): Young growing animals, Osteosarcoma.
    - C-ALP / SIALP (Corticosteroid-induced): **Dogs ONLY** (Hyperadrenocorticism, exogenous steroids). Cats DO NOT produce C-ALP。
  - **Feline Note**: ALP 在貓相對 insensitive；即使是 jaundiced cats 也可能 normal。
- **GGT (Gamma-Glutamyl Transferase)**:
  - **Characteristics**: Microsomal membrane-bound。無 bone isoenzyme。
  - **Species Differences**:
    - Dogs: 比 ALP 更 specific 但 less sensitive。
    - Cats: 對 hepatobiliary diseases 通常比 ALP 更 sensitive。
    - **Exception**: Feline Hepatic Lipidosis 時，ALKP 通常 markedly increased 但 GGT 卻 normal 或 only slightly elevated。

### Liver Function Markers
- **Bilirubin**: Hyperbilirubinaemia 代表 liver dysfunction (需先 rule out pre-hepatic haemolysis)。
- **BUN (Blood Urea Nitrogen)**: Decreased (肝臟無法將 ammonia 轉化為 urea)。
- **Albumin (ALB)**: Decreased (chronic loss of synthetic function，如 PSS, cirrhosis)。
- **Glucose (GLU)**: Decreased (massive necrosis, end-stage liver disease, PSS)。
- **Cholesterol (CHOL)**: 在 EHBDO (Extrahepatic bile duct obstruction) 時 Increased；在 PSS/cirrhosis 時 Decreased。

## 2. Clinical Patterns

### 2.1 Hepatocellular Damage (ALT/AST Dominant)
- **Primary Liver Disease**: Acute/chronic hepatitis, hepatotoxins (如 xylitol, cycad), infectious canine hepatitis.
- **Secondary (Reactive) Hepatopathy**:
  - GI disease (portal delivery of toxins/bacteria).
  - Endocrine (Feline Hyperthyroidism, Diabetes Mellitus).
  - Hypoxia / Systemic inflammation / Sepsis.
- **Drug-Induced**: Anticonvulsants (phenobarbital), corticosteroids.

### 2.2 Cholestasis (ALKP/GGT Dominant)
- **Intrahepatic Cholestasis**: Hepatocyte swelling 壓迫 canaliculi (hepatitis, hepatic lipidosis, neoplasia).
- **Extrahepatic Bile Duct Obstruction (EHBDO)**: Pancreatitis, choleliths, gallbladder mucocoele, biliary carcinoma.
  - **Key Findings**: Acholic faeces, massive bilirubinuria (無 urobilinogen), hypercholesterolaemia.

### 2.3 Feline Hepatic Lipidosis
- **Classic Signature**: Marked ALKP ↑ + Normal/Mild GGT ↑ + Hyperbilirubinaemia.

### 2.4 Diagnostic Approach to Icterus / Jaundice
1. **Pre-hepatic (Hemolytic)**:
   - Marked anaemia (low PCV), regenerative.
   - IMHA, Babesia, oxidative damage (Heinz bodies).
   - Liver enzymes 可能 normal (除非併發 secondary hypoxic injury)。
2. **Hepatic (Hepatocellular / Intrahepatic)**:
   - ALT, AST, ALKP, GGT 皆 increased。
   - Hepatitis, toxicosis, FIP, lipidosis.
3. **Post-hepatic (Obstructive)**:
   - ALKP, GGT 增加幅度大於 ALT, AST (Cholestatic enzymes dominant)。
   - 伴隨 Hypercholesterolaemia。

## 3. Important Artifacts & Considerations
- **Corticosteroids**: 只有 dogs 會誘發 C-ALP (SIALP) 上升。Cats 缺乏此 pathway。
- **Breed Specifics**: Older dogs 常見 nodular hyperplasia，會導致 isolated ALKP elevation 而無 clinical significance。
- **EDTA Contamination**: EDTA 會 chelate 必要的 cofactors，導致 ALKP 測出假性低下 (falsely decreased)。
