# BSAVA Leukocyte Interpretation (LEUKOCYTES.md)

## 1. Physiological Leukocytosis (Excitement)
- **Pattern**: Neutrophilia (no left shift) + Lymphocytosis.
- **Mechanism**: Epinephrine 導致 Marginal pool 進入 Circulating pool。常用於幼犬貓。

## 2. Stress Leukogram (典型糖皮質激素反應)
- **Pattern**: Neutrophilia + **Lymphopenia (最關鍵指標)** + Eosinopenia + Monocytosis (常見於犬)。
- **Clinical Sign**: 代表體內有足夠內源性或外源性 Cortisols。

## 3. Inflammatory Leukogram
- **Neutrophilia + Left Shift (Band Neutrophils ↑)**: 代表骨髓釋放儲存池。
- **Degenerative Left Shift**: Band cells > Mature Neutrophils (代表預後不佳，骨髓無法負荷發炎需求)。
- **Toxic Changes**: Döhle bodies, Cytoplasmic basophilia, Vacuolation (強烈代表嚴重發炎或細菌感染)。

## 4. Persistent Lymphocytosis
- **Differentials**: Hypoadrenocorticism (Addison's - 缺乏 Cortisol 的 Lymphopenia 反應), Chronic antigenic stimulation, Leukemia.
