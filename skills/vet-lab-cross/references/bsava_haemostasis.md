# BSAVA Haemostasis Interpretation (HAEMOSTASIS.md)

## 1. Primary Haemostasis (血小板與血管壁)
- **Clinical Signs**: Petechiae (紫斑), Ecchymoses (瘀斑), Epistaxis (流鼻血)。
- **Indicators**: **Platelet Count ↓** (Thrombocytopenia), BMBT (Buccal Mucosal Bleeding Time) 延長。
- **Differentials**: Destructive (IMTP), Consumptive (DIC), Sequestration (Splenomegaly), or Production failure (Bone marrow).

## 2. Secondary Haemostasis (凝血因子)
- **Clinical Signs**: Haematoma (血腫), Cavitary bleeding (胸腹腔出血)。
- **Indicators**: **aPTT** (Intrinsic/Common), **PT** (Extrinsic/Common) 延長。
- **Patterns**:
    - PT ↑, aPTT normal: 早期滅鼠藥中毒 (Factor VII 最短半衰期)。
    - PT ↑, aPTT ↑: 嚴重滅鼠藥中毒, DIC, Liver Failure。

## 3. Disseminated Intravascular Coagulation (DIC)
- **Indicators**: 
    - Platelets ↓
    - aPTT/PT ↑
    - FDPs/D-dimer ↑
    - Schistocytes (RBC fragments) 觀察。
