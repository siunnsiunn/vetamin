# IDEXX Official Reference Ranges (Catalyst & ProCyte)

## 🐱 Feline (貓) - Adult
### Chemistry (Catalyst)
- **ALB (Albumin)**: 2.2 – 4.0 g/dL
- **ALKP (Alk. Phosphatase)**: 14 – 111 U/L
- **ALT (Alanine Aminotransferase)**: 12 – 130 U/L
- **BUN/UREA**: 16 – 36 mg/dL
- **CREA (Creatinine)**: 0.8 – 2.4 mg/dL
- **GLU (Glucose)**: 74 – 159 mg/dL
- **TP (Total Protein)**: 5.7 – 8.9 g/dL
- **SDMA**: 0 – 14 μg/dL

### Hematology (ProCyte)
- **RBC**: 6.54 – 12.20 M/μL
- **HCT (Hematocrit)**: 30.3 – 52.3 %
- **HGB (Hemoglobin)**: 9.8 – 16.2 g/dL
- **WBC**: 2.87 – 17.02 K/μL
- **PLT (Platelets)**: 151 – 600 K/μL

## 🐶 Canine (犬) - Adult
### Chemistry (Catalyst)
- **ALB**: 2.3 – 4.0 g/dL
- **ALKP**: 23 – 212 U/L
- **ALT**: 10 – 125 U/L
- **BUN/UREA**: 7 – 27 mg/dL
- **CREA**: 0.5 – 1.8 mg/dL
- **GLU**: 74 – 143 mg/dL
- **TP**: 5.2 – 8.2 g/dL
- **SDMA**: 0 – 14 μg/dL

### Hematology (ProCyte)
- **RBC**: 5.65 – 8.87 M/μL
- **HCT**: 37.3 – 61.7 %
- **HGB**: 13.1 – 20.5 g/dL
- **WBC**: 5.05 – 16.76 K/μL
- **PLT**: 148 – 484 K/μL

## ⚠️ Geriatric Note (高齡差異)
- **Canine Geriatric**: CREA range remains similar (0.5-1.8), but ALB lower limit slightly lower (2.2).
- **Feline Geriatric**: CREA upper limit might be slightly more sensitive.
