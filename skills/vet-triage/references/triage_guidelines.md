# Veterinary Triage Guidelines

Based on: "Basic triage in dogs and cats: Part I-III" (Thomovsky E, Ilie L; Can Vet J 2024)

## Triage Categories

| Category | Description | Clinical Signs / Conditions |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **RED** | **Immediate** | Respiratory distress, Shock, Profuse hemorrhage, Aortic thromboembolism, Actively seizing, Unresponsive, Cluster seizures, Acute abnormal behavior, Acute continuous vocalization, Rapid onset of abdominal distention (GDV suspicion), Presenting fetal parts, Suspicion for hypoglycemia, Body temp > 105.5°F or < 96°F, Evisceration, Envenomation, Proptosed eye, Penetrating or acute chemical ocular injury. |
| **ORANGE** | **Very Urgent** | Acute stridor, Moderate respiratory distress, Subcutaneous emphysema, Uncontrollable major bleeding, Pale mucous membranes (in absence of shock), Abdominal fluid wave, Altered level of consciousness, Acute complete loss of vision, Toxin ingestion, Possible foreign body ingestion > 24 h with anorexia or vomiting, Active labor, Petechia/ecchymosis, Suspected hyperglycemia and ketosis, Severe pain, Generalized weakness, Severe dehydration > 8%, Rapid onset of testicular swelling and pain, Urethral obstruction. |
| **YELLOW** | **Urgent** | Mild respiratory distress, Uncontrollable minor bleeding, Acute spinal/peripheral deficits or acute deterioration, Head tilt, Possible foreign body ingestion, Persistent vomiting, Melena, Oral trauma, Open fractures, Medium to large skin wounds, Abnormal vaginal blood loss in pregnant patient, Hematuria without stranguria, Ventroflexion of the head and neck, Moderate pain, Severe pruritus. |
| **GREEN** | **Non-Urgent** | Coughing, Heart murmur, Recent isolated seizure, Vomiting, Stranguria/tenesmus, Local inflammation, Recent mild pain and pruritus. |

## Quick Assessment Flowchart

1. **Difficulty breathing?**
   - YES: Provide Oxygen. Stabilize as per Respiratory Distress protocol.
   - NO: Proceed to step 2.
2. **Cardiovascularly stable?** (Shock? Bleeding? Arrhythmia?)
   - NO: Treat as per Shock/Bleeding/Arrhythmia protocol.
   - YES: Proceed to step 3.
3. **Active seizures?**
   - YES: Treat as per Seizure protocol.
   - NO: Proceed to step 4.
4. **Walking/Standing up?**
   - NO: Perform neurological and orthopedic assessment.
   - YES: Perform complete physical examination.
